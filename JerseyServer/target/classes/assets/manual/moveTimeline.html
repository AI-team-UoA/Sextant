Interacting with the Timeline

There are three ways to move the Timeline band:

  • We can hold down mouse left button at any point on the Timeline and move right or left.

  • We can use the button to move left and the button to move right. When using these buttons the Timeline moves to the selected direction by the amount of days specified in the box that is located below the move buttons.

  • We can use the button to make the Timeline move every 1 second by the amount of days specified in the box that is located below the move buttons. The button then switches to button that pauses the Timeline at the current position.

When we move the Timeline we affect the visibility of any temporal layers that are loaded. The features of a temporal layer are shown on the map when their temporal dimension intersects with the top band's span. Otherwise the layer is hidden. The visibility of a temporal layer in response to the Timeline span does not affect the layer's show/hide checkbox in the Layers panel.
